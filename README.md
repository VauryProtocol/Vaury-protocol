# Vaury Protocol

> **Unlock Liquidity Without Selling. Borrow Against Your PumpSwap Positions on Solana.**

Vaury Protocol is a decentralized lending protocol built on Solana that enables users to borrow against their PumpSwap LP positions and pump.fun ecosystem assets without selling their holdings. By transforming liquidity positions into productive collateral, Vaury unlocks instant capital for borrowers while allowing lenders to earn sustainable yield from secured lending markets.

Designed for the next generation of Solana DeFi, Vaury combines efficient capital utilization, transparent lending, and on-chain collateral management into a seamless borrowing experience. Its public description emphasizes borrowing against PumpSwap tokens, unlocking liquidity without selling, and providing sustainable lender yield on Solana. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
